Usability & Accessibility

There were many concerns in the development of this website. However, the main focus is to ensure that all audience members will have an enjoyable and comfortable experience while using this site. That is why the page is designed to help potential users who may have disabilities and impairments. This webpage could also be utilized from most browser platforms and is mobile friendly.

  • Structure

    • Users can easily distinguish when a section begins and ends. Exploring through the site is effortless and convenient with the help of the navigation bar. Links to other pages are clear and properly placed. Blank space was removed to maximized screen usage.

  • Color & Font

    • The color scheme on this page ensures that each section is recognizable. All text is defaulted to be white and placed on a dark background to prevent any issues that relates to vision.
    The text font style and size legible for most users. Features were include to hide text if users desired a clearer interface.
